Sify Discord Bot - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024-01-05

At Sify, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with the Sify Discord bot. By using Sify, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Sify collects the following types of data when you use the bot for moderation purposes:

  • Server Data: Server IDs, channel IDs, role information, and user permissions required to moderate the server effectively.
  • User Data: Usernames, User IDs, and message content for moderation actions (e.g., bans, mutes, kicks).
  • Logs: Logs of moderation actions, such as who performed an action (e.g., muting, banning) and when it occurred.

2. How We Use Your Data

Sify uses the data collected to perform moderation tasks and ensure a safe and well-organized Discord community. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Log moderation actions like muting, kicking, or banning users for server transparency.
  • Track infractions and assist with dispute resolution between server members.
  • Store moderation logs for server owners and administrators to review.

3. Data Sharing

Sify does not share your data with third parties, except under the following circumstances:

  • If required by law or to comply with legal processes.
  • If necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Sify, its users, or the public.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously and employ reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of data transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

Sify retains collected data (such as moderation logs) for as long as necessary to provide the moderation services for your server. If the bot is removed from your server, the associated data may be deleted at the discretion of the server owner.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data Sify has collected about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data if it is inaccurate or no longer needed.

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the official support server or email provided below.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and your continued use of Sify after changes are made indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us via our official Discord support server or email at https://discord.gg/xvwtWX8RTh.
